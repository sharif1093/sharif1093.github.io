Biography

Mohammadreza Sharif is a Ph.D. candidate in RIVeR lab, Northeastern University. His research focuses on developing human-robot interaction algorithms. He joined Northeastern University back in 2015. Being an enthusiastic programmer, he has developed several packages and frameworks for deep reinforcement learning, deep learning, and robotics.
